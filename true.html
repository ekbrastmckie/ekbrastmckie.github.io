The Truth


The Truth is that It just doesn't matter.
Nothing really matters; nothing is really important.

and the Truth about that is
that any deviation in belief from
this universal Truth is merely self-important delusion.


This is not to suggest that we should value nothing. On the contrary, our Values are what define each of our individual Characters.
John values honesty and integrity. Therefore, he determines a human being's Value in accordance with his perception of that person's honesty and integrity. This determination is applied
equally to himself.
So, John's image of his own Value (self-worth, self-esteem), and, actually, the entirety of his self-Image is based upon his perception of his relationship to his Values.
John's Mind is quite like a map of all his Images. So, his Image of himself exists in relation to all of his other Images.
