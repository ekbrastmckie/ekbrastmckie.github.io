The Text

1 The light shines backward from my eyes

2 My mind shines backward into the past

3 Everything in it is illusory; though it appears to be present, it is not.

4 The light is present.

5 It is the light of existence, which shines in the abyss.


1. The Light(4, 5) may be taken literally, as electromagnetic radiation, or as symbolic of the Real as a whole.
The Light merely appears to "shine[] backward from [the] eyes." See point 2. On further reflection, I have come to believe that this line is perhaps nothing more than poetic edifice.

2. Mind forever dwells on memory. Even a direct experience can only come so close to the present [keep reading]. Light takes time to reach the eye. Mind takes time to create an image.
The one thing we experience in real time is Mind. Mind is our only experience and it is present. If Mind could be purified, freed from abstraction,
perhaps suffering would slough off.

3. Actually, Mind is present. Only, it is not what it appears to be, which is external and Real. Mind is an image of the Real, produced by some particular processes.

4. All that is Real is present. The light is Real. The Mind is Real. The Mind is not the light. The Mind only appears to be the light.
